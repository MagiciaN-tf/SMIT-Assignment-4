Maggi Noodles

Maggi is a quick and delicious noodle dish that's perfect for a fast meal. Hre's a simple recipe:

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Maggi noodles
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • Maggi masala (included in the pack)

Method

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot and add Maggi noodles. Cook until noodles are soft, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a separate pan, heat vegetable oil and saut√© chopped onions until golden brown.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and mixed vegetables. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Now, add the boiled Maggi noodles to the vegetables.
  5. Sprinkle the Maggi masala over the noodles and mix well.
  6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Your delicious Maggi noodles are ready to be served!

Notes

Maggi Masala:
Adjust the quantity of Maggi masala according to your taste preferences.
customize:
Feel free to customize your Maggi by adding ingredients like boiled eggs,boiled chicken, or cheese.
